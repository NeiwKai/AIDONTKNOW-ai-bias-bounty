## Bias detection with AIF360

> *__Disclaimer__*
> | DI |  |
> |--|--|
> | 1 | No bias |
> | <1 | Has Bias against topic |
> | >1 | Has Bias against the opposite of topic |  
> 
> | SPD | |
> |--|--|
> | 0 | No bias |
> | <0 | Has Bias against topic |
> | >1 | Has Bias against the opposite of topic |
